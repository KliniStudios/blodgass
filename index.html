04 · Compensation

How the body answers back

A primary acid–base disturbance triggers a predictable counter-response from whichever system isn't the primary problem — lungs answer the kidneys, kidneys answer the lungs. The body never fully overshoots into the opposite abnormality, and it rarely restores pH all the way to a perfect 7.40.
